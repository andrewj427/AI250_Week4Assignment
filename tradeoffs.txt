Tradeoffs between Local and Cloud AI hosting


When using a cloud API, you do not have to worry about your machine's power. you simply call on the API, which does all of the calculations in a server somewhere across the world, and gives you an output which you can use in your program. this saves you computational power, but can be costly to maintain an API token, and you cannot train with your own data, if that's something you want to do. When you run the AI locally, like with Llama3, you are using your own computers resources, but do not need to worry about token usage. it also allows you to train with your own data.
